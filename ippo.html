Hajime No ippo

This show is nearly perfect—10/10. Ippo's journey from a nobody to a skilled boxer, along with well-developed side characters, is incredible. Even the opponent boxers have backstories that make them relatable. The fight choreography and build-ups were top-notch, especially for its time. If you haven't seen it, I highly recommend it—one of the best sports anime out there! That video captures the emotion of the fights perfectly.
